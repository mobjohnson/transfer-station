Portfolio



Senatorial

Know what your Senators are saying!

Senatorial finds the names of the two senators for each of the two states in the query, and then creates a line chart showing how many times each senator has used that phrase over the previous two years in a congressional speech or committee meeting.

Live App » GitHub »



Mappetizers

Want to have fun with your friends?

Mappetizers is a group project that allows a user to organize an outing with friends. After entering a city and date range, a map shows available events. After selecting the event for the outing and providing friends' email addresses, your friends receive an email invitation to the outing with a link the the event on EventBrite.

About Me

I was a Civil Engineer who had started in design, but had migrated up the corporate management path. Although I always performed billable design work, I was predominantly managing Engineers and sub-consultants.

While listening to the radio, I heard the NPR Marketplace segment about Patsy Price. A Google engineer who had ended up in management, she wanted to return to design and did WDI. I had done programming over the years, but nothing on the Web. I realized WDI would restart my career.


First I took the Back-End Web Development part-time course at General Assembly in DC. Then Web Development Immersive - Twelve weeks. All day. Every day.

For me, WDI was an incredible adventure with extraordinary classmates that led to a place of wide-open possibilities.

Now I'm ready to use what I have learned. Please check out my resume, or take a look at my projects. Thanks!!!
