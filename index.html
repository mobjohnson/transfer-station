Senatorial

Know what your Senators are saying!

Senatorial retrieves the two senators for each of the two states in the query, and then creates a graph showing how many times each senator has used a phrase over the previous six months in a congressional speech or committee meeting.

Mappetizers

Want to have fun with your friends?

Mappetizers is a group project that allows a user to organize an outing with friends. After entering a city and date range, a map shows available events. After selecting the event for the outing and providing friends' email addresses, your friends receive an email invitation to the outing with a link the the event on EventBrite.

NextBooks

What books should your read next?

NextBooks recommends books you should read, based upon previous books read. For example, it helps you to read the Jack Reacher novels in order. After providing a Collection name (for example, Sci-fi) and the Authors belonging to your collection (Michael Connelly and Lee Child), you are given the next book written by each author.
