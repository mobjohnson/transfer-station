Overnighter

Time to go camping!!!

This is my first web app, from the General Assembly - Back End Web Development part-time course in the summer of 2015.

Overnighter allows Scouts and Scouters to share their favorite camping spots with other Troops. It includes activities associated with camp sites and rank advancement, and provides for commenting.

NextBooks

What books should your read next?

This is from the twelve-week Web Development Immersive course at General Assembly in the fall of 2015.

NextBooks recommends books you should read, based upon books you have already read. For example, it helps you to read the Jack Reacher novels in order. After providing a Collection name (for example, Mystery) and the Authors belonging to your collection (Michael Connelly and Lee Child), you are given the next book written by each author.
